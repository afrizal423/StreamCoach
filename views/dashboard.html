Frequently Asked Questions

Transparency about your data and security.

Is my API Key safe? Where is it stored?

Yes, absolutely. StreamCoach AI follows a "Bring Your Own Key" (BYOK) model. Your Gemini API Key is stored locally in your browser's LocalStorage. It is never saved to our database or servers. It is only sent temporarily to Google's servers to authenticate your specific analysis request.

Do you keep my video files?

No. Your video is uploaded temporarily for processing (extracting audio and frames) and is immediately deleted from our temporary storage once the analysis is complete. We value your privacy and intellectual property.

Is this free to use?

The StreamCoach tool itself is free. However, since you use your own Google Gemini API Key, usage will count towards your own quota. Google offers a generous free tier for Gemini Flash, which should cover many hours of analysis.
